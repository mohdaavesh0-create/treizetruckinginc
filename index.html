About Us

Your Trusted Partner in Truck Dispatching

Treize Trucking Inc is a leading truck dispatching company based in Manteca, California, providing comprehensive dispatching services across North America. Founded by Amarjit Singh, we have built our reputation on reliability, efficiency, and unwavering commitment to our clients' success.

Our experienced dispatch team works around the clock to ensure your trucks are always loaded with the best-paying freight. We leverage cutting-edge technology and industry relationships to negotiate top rates and optimize every route.

Our Services

Comprehensive Dispatching Solutions

End-to-end dispatching services designed to keep your trucks moving and your business growing.

Load Planning & Booking

Our expert dispatchers find the highest-paying loads that match your equipment, preferred lanes, and schedule. We negotiate aggressively to get you the best rates.

Most Popular

Route Optimization

We plan the most efficient routes using advanced technology, minimizing deadhead miles and fuel costs while maximizing your profitability on every trip.

Rate Negotiation

Leveraging our carrier relationships and market expertise, we negotiate top dollar rates for every load, ensuring you get paid what you deserve.

Fleet Management

Complete oversight of your fleet operations including scheduling, maintenance reminders, compliance tracking, and performance analytics.

Invoice & Paperwork

We handle all the back-office work — invoices, bills of lading, rate confirmations, and payment follow-ups — so you can focus on driving.

Insurance & Compliance

Stay compliant with FMCSA regulations and DOT requirements. We help you navigate the complex world of trucking insurance and permits.
